🌱 IoT Agricultural Data Collection System

A smart monitoring system designed to collect real-time soil and climate data for smallholder farmers in Busia County, Kenya — laying the foundation for a future AI-powered agricultural advisory system.

🎯 Project Objective

Design and develop an IoT-based data collection infrastructure to serve as the backbone for a future AI-powered agricultural advisory system for smallholder farmers.

📡 What We Monitor

  • 🌡 Temperature (°C)
  • 💧 Relative Humidity (%)
  • 🌱 Soil Moisture (%)
